Biography

Currently I am a first year M.Sc. student of School of Software Engineering (National Exemplary School of Software) in University of Science and Technology of China and a member of Data Intelligence Research Center, led by associate professor Yang Wang.

I got my B.Sc. degree in School of Computer Science in June 2022 from Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc degree in University of Science and Technology of China without entrance examination.
