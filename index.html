Biography

Currently I am a second year M.Sc. student of School of Software Engineering in University of Science and Technology of Chin and a member of Data Intelligence Research Center, led by associate professor Yang Wang .

I got my B.Sc. degree in School of Computer Science in June 2022 from Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology, supervised by Professor Xiaolong Xu. In the same year, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc degree in University of Science and Technology of China without entrance examination.

I once experienced as Research Intern at Tencent Youtu Laboratory (Shanghai, 2023.4~2023.9) and Sensedeal (Nanjing, 2021.10~2022.5). In Sensedeal, heartfelt thanks to Dr. Cangqi Zhou for guiding me in deep learning. His mentorship laid a strong foundation in mathematics and coding skills for me, a rare opportunity for future stages. In Tencent, thanks to Lei Shen for pushing me to complete papers.
